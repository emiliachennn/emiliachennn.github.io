About Us

Vysara / About Us

Vysara was created just recently in 2025 of May. Vysara is a jewelry brand thats like no other, where its like a magical portal to a complete new world of charm. It all began with the vision of creating jewelry pieces that would make any who wore them feel like they're in a fairytale. The name "Vysara" was carefully picked to show a sense of mystery elegance, and allure, just like our jewelry pieces. The name combines the two words; Vivid, and Stellar. The inspiration for creating Vysara was simply when I had one of those dreams where I never wanted to wake up. There were beautiful butterflies flying around, colored flowers, and a mythical forest. The brand draws on this inspiration and each piece is crafted to look as if it was plucked from a dream.

About the Founder

The founder of Vysara, Emilia, has always been inspired by the beauty of nature and the magic of dreams. With a background in design and a passion for storytelling, Emilia envisioned a jewelry brand that would bring fairytales to life. Each piece is a reflection of her creativity, dedication, and love for crafting timeless treasures. Emilia believes that jewelry is more than just an accessory—it’s a way to express individuality and capture the essence of a moment.
